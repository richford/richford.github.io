Hi, I’m Adam

I’m a postdoctoral scholar in Developmental-Behavioral Pediatrics at Stanford University. and an affiliate at the University of Washington eScience Institute. I develop statistical learning techniques and software for the analysis of neuroimaging data. I also develop web-based assessments for the deep phenotyping of dyslexia.
